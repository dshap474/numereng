# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or hateful conduct.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior on GitHub, use GitHub's built-in reporting tools for abusive content or users.

For project-specific conduct concerns that do not need platform moderation, open an issue only if you are comfortable making the report publicly visible. Include links, timestamps, and any relevant context.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban users who violate this Code of Conduct.
